poly2lsf

Calculates the line spectral frequencies for the given prediction polynomial

Calling Sequence

lsf = poly2lsf(a)
poly2lsf maps Prediction polynomial coefficients to line spectral frequencies.

Parameters

a:

Coefficients of prediction polynomial(the roots of the polynomial has to be inside the unity circle)

lsf:

Line spectral frequencies

Example

X=[1 0.6149 0.9899 0 0.0031 -0.0082] 
lsf = poly2lsf(X)
EXPECTED OUTPUT:
lsf  =0.7841731  1.5605415  1.8776459  1.8984313  2.3592523
